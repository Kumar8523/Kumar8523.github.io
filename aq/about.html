About AquaWorld

Your trusted source for premium aquarium supplies

Our Story

Founded in 2010, AquaWorld began as a small family-owned fish store with a passion for aquatic life. What started as a humble operation has grown into a premier destination for aquarium enthusiasts across the country.

Our founder, marine biologist David Wilson, wanted to create a place where both beginners and experienced aquarists could find quality products and expert advice.

Today, we're proud to serve thousands of customers with our carefully curated selection of tropical fish, premium fish food, and high-quality aquarium accessories.

Our Mission

At AquaWorld, we're committed to providing the highest quality aquatic life and supplies while promoting responsible fish keeping practices.

We believe that aquariums should be sustainable ecosystems that bring joy to their owners while providing healthy environments for fish and aquatic plants.

Our team of aquatic specialists is dedicated to helping you create and maintain beautiful underwater worlds through our products, advice, and ongoing support.
